Our Story

Authentic Taste Meets Rustic Charm

Little Village is more than just a restaurant; it's a journey back to the roots. Inspired by the traditional lifestyle of Andhra villages, our restaurant offers a peaceful escape from the city's hustle. With mud walls, wooden seating, and open-air ambiance, we bring you the true essence of village dining combined with premium multi-cuisine flavors.
